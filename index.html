Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock


As explained by Sheldon Cooper in the famous series Big Bang Theory; Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock is a extension of Rock, Paper Scissors.

The rules of the game are: Paper beats Rock, Rock beats Scissors, Scissors cut the Paper, Spock vaporises Rock, Paper disapproves Spock, Lizard poisons Spock, Rock crushes the Lizard, Spock smashes the Scissor, Scissor decapitates the Lizard, Lizard eats the Paper
